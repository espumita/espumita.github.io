Book notes: Soft Skills, The software developer's life manual

Posted on 2020-08-13 In Development

Reading this book has been an interesting experience. This book can give you some light to many diferent topics in a software developer life. John Z. Sonmez tells his story. You can learn very interesting lessons. I recomend not only read the book, but also discover his story the years after writing it.

As always, if you want to go in deep in the topics in the book, I recomend to you to adquire it and help the author.

Career

Business mindset. You as a profesional ofer a service or a product to a client. Can you describe this service in a sentece? You should treating you carrer like a business. Had you ever thought in how do you offer thease service to your clients? What they expect?

What are your goals in you carrer? What about next 5 or 10 years? Are you making steps to this goals every day or to other direction? You should keep this goals in mind everyday, becouse the easy path is to stay in the “confort zone” and live in an horizotnal aimlessly way. Take care that making steps whithout objetives can make you walk in circles.

Quit your job and find a new one if you cannot make thease goals’ steps in that job.

People skills are more important than you think. Think in all the interactions you got during the day: apointments, emails, discusions, Slack, documentation, etc. This profesion is more about dealing with people and situations, than with code.

Boost your oportunities in interview process. Find companies that have problems that you can solve. Networking its very important, becouse when you are a good profesional and make a good work, your teamates and all the profesionals/friends you know can refer you. Be refered put you in a diferent position in a selection process, but take in acount that they provally expect more about you. Its important to be yourself, selection process are about finding the right person for the job, not the most smart.

There are many options to work as a software developer: Frelancer, independient consultant, consultant, developer in a company, create your product, create your startup, etc. Take all the options in mind.

You should specialize at some area. When you speciaize you close some doors but open many other ones. Think about tools, technologies and languajes. But, focus on a specif area, for example: backend development, mobile development, etc.

There are many types of companies: small/startup, medium, large. And there is a big diferencie betwren a software develop company and company with software developers. Think about what fits you.

Promotions are based on visiblity and good work. For example, make weekly report. To get promoted or to just improve as profesional you can do things like: Teaching or mentor others, make talks, take more responsability, improve your skills, inprove the team, improve the product, or be a problem solver. Some companies has politic stuff but this is less important.

Being profesional is diferent to be an amateur developer, do what is right. Learn to say no. Learn to priorize and to act. Don’t depend on others, be profesional. Don’t fall into no exit ways. Keep your freedom and authonomy, don’t get condicioned or controlled. This hanppends when you cannot say no, for example when you have to make a desition about generate technical debt caused by deadline. Find a balance from quality vs self improvement you have to be very carefully to decide why and how you can decrease your quality, thease depends on the context and what do you can improve instead.

Know all your profesional options. If you want to change your parth, for example to freelancing, or to live from a product builded by yourself, make a parallel change. This is important, becouse you can think that if you where not working you will have 40 hours per week to spend on other side projects, but this is not true, your productive time will never be 40 hours. Remember that you have to pay the bills. Take in acount contract clausules with a company when trying to swap thease kinds of projects.

Freelancer. How much should you charge to your clients for the work. Probally the double or triple than a normal job. Becouse you have to pay taxes, lawyers, economic administrators, you have to pay yourself your equipment, etc… Remember that you probally will not work 40 hours, and you will have periods without clients or doing other stuff related to the job but not for clients.

Building your own product. Search your audience first, then find their problems, and then the solution. Make sure to do thinks like preorder programs, becouse someone have to pay that invested effort. Test first the idea, make fast feedback loops, Lean Startup. Take care to enter a game with much competitors.

Creating a startup. What will be the startup type: created to grow with inversionss or one boostraped, created and financed by the founders. Think about the startup inversion cicle: Inital Seed with Ángels investors or next iterations with capital investors. What is the startup economic payday plan? Get arquired in a big pay day, wich is the normal, or to generate a profitable business. The more founders, the less benefists and desition power. Take care with the % of the company that is given to the inversors.

Remote work. Take care with, schedule, distractions, loneliness and motivation.

Fake untill you make it. Probally you are not the profesional that you whant to be at 100% today.. So you should act as the person you want to be, untill you became it. The power of the mind is important here.

Don’t waste your time making the perfect resume. Its a skill that you will use few time in you career, compared with the real work time. So don’t waste time improving this skill. It all depends on the company you want to enter, but, if you really need an efective resume consider to pay to get it generated by a profesional.

In case you want to create it yourself, remember that companies need personal to work on problems, its important wich of thease problems you can solve or had been solved and what actions has you performed to solve them, pay atention to details, and think diferent.

Open your mind in the tech world, do not be dogmatic ever, this will only limit yourself, never be religious about frameworks, technologies or languajes.

Marketing yourself

You should treating you carrer like a business, and yourself as a brand.

Find your niche, thinks about what value or entertiment you can give to others.

Making a talks, blog, podcast, screencast, youtube, etc. are well methods now a days to have a precense and to generate value to others. If you want to became relevant, with the time in some area, you have to create an strategy. Expose your talent is equal or more important than having it.

You are a brand consider, find your niche, tagline, elevator pitch, visuals, etc. The same in social networks.

Public speaking. Remember that the people remembers the concerts not the thimes they reproduce a song in Spotify. You should consider speak at conferences.

Do not be afraid to look like an idiot when you start, all good people start for something. Whit the time your skills will improve. You will have to deal with positive and negative opinions during the process, it will be hard. But its important to keep in mind that take the steep to start is the most important one, and is what normaly people don’t dare to.

Learning

Transform your learning path in a game, caouse playing a game is most easy. Think about a topic, then find the 20% of knowledge you need to solve the 80% of the problems. You don’t need to know in deep evrything, even a complete book, even thease post. You don’t need to consume what you don’t need.

Ten steps strategy:

  1. Get the big picture
  2. Determine scope
  3. Define success
  4. Find resources
  5. Create a learning plan
  6. Filter resources
  7. Learn enough to get started (repeat this steps for each module)
  8. Play around
  9. Learn enough to do something useful
  10. Teach

Learn to teach and learn to explain will make you improve your understand avility for things.

Fill gabs in your knowledge of the tasks you usually do can help you to be much more productive and do much more with less.

Financial

Find assets and avoid liabilities. Get much money doesn’t necesary means economic freedom. Take in acount inflaction when you think in long term.

Stocks, options and futures, real state investment, pasives incomes. You should know a bit about all the options and the benefists and drawbacks of all of them.

When tallking about a jobs offers. Know first your numbers, then the company numbers. If you want to enter in the game of negotioations you must know very well the strengs and weak points, be in a disadvantage position is usually a bad point of start.

What about your retirement plan? had you ever thought about? had you thought when you want to retire and how much you need to generate to do that? How some people get retired at 33? How can be thease posible? Is this your goal? Had you ever thought how retirements plans works in other countries?

Debts are evil. Paying an interest directly means pay to other one to make him rich. Take care with interests, comon failures when dealing with multiple debts are:

  1. Having a debts and trying to save money at the same time.
  2. Debts are not priorized based on the type of interest. The higher the interest is the most it should be priorized.
  3. Unnecesarry debts. Example: buying a car. If you want to be 5/6 years paying a car, why not save that money for the car and buy it in 5/6 years instead of paying a bill without interest. You save much money in the long term.
  4. Taking debt without thinking in how and when paying it. If its a debts that is not goin to give you an economic advantage in the future, maybe you should not take it.

Retirement at 33 book example: get enought pasive incomes untill you don’t need a 40 hours work. Priorize debts and reduce as much interest as you can. Invest in real state. Take the oportunities that only happend once in life.

Spirit

The power of the mind. If you are a positive person, thease will increase your health and you will have a better life with much more posibilities, oportunities, and chance to success. Mindfulness. Think about the placebo efect. When you act as you will like, your mind will control your body to make it posible. Don’t be a victim of each situation, the world doesn’t conspire againts you. Only you with your mind can change things. Things happend, but you don’t allready know if something is god or bad in first instance. Create a positive mind.

If your objetive is to fight with mediocrity, don’t put rocks in your way. Stop telling you canno do something, or you are bad in something. The bigger enemy you have to defeat will be you.

Build a strong self image. Had you ever thought in how you act depending on the clothes you wear? We act diferent, becouse or mind has a mindset acording to the clothes. The same happends when our body changes due to an operation. We act diferent. This is in our subconscious. So thease is the same, but at a mental level. Your self image should work for you. Act as the person you want to be some day until you be that person.

Don’t have fear to fail. A fail is not a defeat, only if you don’t want that will be the end. Most people that has making a step of success in life, comes from the worst fail step in life.

Force yourself to go through uncomfortable situations to get more a more better in things, deal with confort.

Decide what life you want to live, and go for it.
